Our Story

Boba Bear was founded in order to bring the joy of bubble tea to everyone in our local community. Located in San Francisco, we started as a small passion project which has now grown into a local shop known for our high-quality, locally sourced ingredients and a commitment to sustainability.

We take pride in crafting new-style teas and boba drinks that not only taste amazing but also mirror the traditional tastes of Chinese teas. From jasmine milk teas to adventurous new fruit tea flavors, our drinks are made with passion and love.
